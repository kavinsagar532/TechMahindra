Egg Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon butter or oil

Instructions

  1. Beat eggs with salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  4. Cook until set, then fold and serve.
